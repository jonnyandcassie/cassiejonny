As a Transatlantic couple we were always going to make things difficult for half of our combined friends and family - but to ease the pain, we're going to have an event in the UK for anyone who can't make it across the pond. It's going to be a low-key dinner at The Wizard in Alderley Edge, a country pub in the heart of Cheshire's stockbroker belt... Transport should be easy, since Alderley Edge is just a short taxi ride away from Wilmslow and Macclesfield, both of which are served by Virgin Trains from London Euston; half an hour on the train from central Manchester; and twenty minutes on the train from Manchester Airport.* As for accommodation, the Alderley Edge Premier Inn is fairly cheap, as are both The Croft and the Heather Lee Guest House in Wilmslow; otherwise there's always Manchester proper. The dinner itself will be at 7pm on Saturday 15th June; the following morning we might meet up for a hike around the nearby sandstone escarpment. If you think you can make it, please make sure to RSVP.

*There's also a connecting train from Wilmslow to Alderley Edge, so in principle you can get from Euston to Alderley Edge in just 2 hours and 16 mins.
