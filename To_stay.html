We recommend staying at the The Quadrangle Club, which is conveniently situated immediately above the reception and very close to all proceedings. If interested, make sure to mention you're coming for the Betts/Thakkar wedding when you book. Another option is to stay more centrally downtown in the Hyatt Regency Chicago, much bigger and shinier and closer to the bustling tourist action but also closer to expensive parking garages. There are also zillions of other hotels in the city, some of which we are familiar with. If you're interested in any other ideas, feel free to email us.

A quick note on getting to Chicago: for those of you who have not visited before, Chicago has two airports, Midway (which is served mostly by AirTran and Southwest) and O'Hare, which is served by everyone else and is an unfortunate 45 minute drive from the more exciting bits of the weekend). For domestic flights we would recommend Midway for proximity and convenience; international flights go through O'Hare. Also, Chicago is a mere 7-hour drive from Pittsburgh and 3.5 from Ann Arbor, as Cassie well knows. Fear not.
