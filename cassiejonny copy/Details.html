Note that parking nearest to Bond Chapel is on the street and is often metered. We would recommend parking to the south, on the Midway, since it is not metered and should be relatively available on a Saturday afternoon. There is also a free parking garage at 55th & Ellis.

One other thing to keep in mind about Bond Chapel is that it is not air-conditioned and this will be late June in the Midwest. You might consider dressing accordingly; we will do our best to provide fans.

Contra dancing is a bit like English ballroom dancing and a bit like country square dancing, which seemed like a fitting mix. You'll have a new partner every dance, you'll be taught every move you need to know, and you'll have more fun that you probably thought you would.

In contra dancing, your feet are never asked to do more than walk to the music. Each dance is taught by the caller before it is danced and the caller continues to prompt the dancers as needed. Because the pattern of moves of each contra dance is repeated often, the dances are easy to learn. Both beginning and experienced dancers happily share the same set.

The best way to learn more, is to find one near you and go.
